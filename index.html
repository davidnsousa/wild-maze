📖 HOW TO PLAY

You are an animal lost in a wild landscape, searching for food hidden somewhere in the grid. You can only see the numbers on the 6 hexagons next to you. An operation is shown — apply it to your current number and tap the right neighbour. Find your way to the hidden goal!

Before each game, you get to look around first. Numbers are hidden until you tap "I'm Ready!" — then the timer starts.

Difficulty Levels ⭐–⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Each animal has a star rating. Higher levels add new challenges — the info card always explains what's new. Harder animals earn more points per step.

⛓ Op Chain — two operations shown (e.g. "+5 then ×3"). Calculate both steps mentally.
🔄 Reverse — shown as "×? = 12". Find the missing operand in the neighbours.
🌫 Fog — neighbour numbers disappear after 2 seconds. Work fast!

🎒 Abilities

Finish a game fast to earn an ability — the faster your average step time, the better. Abilities persist across sessions and can be used mid-game from the ability bar.

🤖 Auto — auto-taps the correct next cell. Earned avg <4s/step.
🧊 Freeze — pauses the timer until you tap. Earned avg <8s/step.
❤️ Life — silently absorbs your next error. Earned avg <14s/step.

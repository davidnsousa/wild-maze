Wild Maze — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Wild Maze is a free browser-based game developed by David Sousa. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data We Collect

Wild Maze does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Local Storage

Wild Maze saves game progress (best scores, unlocked animals, achievements, and collected finds) locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Third-Party Services

Wild Maze does not use any third-party services, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Wild Maze is suitable for all ages. Since we collect no personal data, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at davidnsousa.github.io/wild-maze.
